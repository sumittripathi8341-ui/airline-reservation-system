💸 Refund Policy

This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions under which refunds are processed for flight bookings made through our system.

  • Cancellation Eligibility: Refunds are applicable only for tickets that are cancelled within the allowed time as per airline rules.
  • Airline Policy: Refund amount and eligibility depend on the respective airline’s cancellation and refund policies.
  • Processing Time: Refunds are typically processed within 5–7 working days after successful cancellation.
  • Payment Method: The refund will be credited back to the original payment method (UPI, Wallet, Net Banking, etc.).
  • Non-Refundable Tickets: Certain promotional or discounted tickets may be non-refundable.
  • Partial Refund: In some cases, only a partial refund may be issued after deducting cancellation charges.
  • Service Charges: Platform or service fees (if any) may not be refundable.
  • Refund Status: Users can track refund status using their PNR or booking details.
  • Exceptional Cases: Refunds due to flight cancellations, delays, or technical issues will be handled as per airline guidelines.

By booking tickets through our platform, you agree to this Refund Policy.
